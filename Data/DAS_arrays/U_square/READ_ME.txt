The U-square arrays are 2 synthetics arrays in Brussels (Belgium) to play with small array and get used to the code. 

It contains 2 arrays:
1. a horizontal fiber with a 90° turn that dives in a 100m deep borehole 
2. a surface fiber that make a loop 
3. A single channel to play with the velocity model and see the sensitivity around the fiber

In both spatial sampling is set to 1m

The model space should be: 
Altitude [-0.2, 0.2] 
longitude [50.8228677, 50.8213637]
latitude [4.3847586, 4.3887782]
